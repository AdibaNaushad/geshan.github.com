Kolaveri Di is a song from a tamil flick named "3", it released on 16-November-2011 and became viral in social media mainly the videos on Youtube. Without doubts, it is a sensation that has more than 1.1 million views/listens on youtube in less than 3 weeks. Kolaveri Di means “why do you have this murderous rage against me?”  as per the composer Anirudh Ravichander who is doing his debut.


The song is an easygoing, bindass, baal chhaina kind song. It has the feel good factor about it but when you listen to it for the first time it seems bad. Similar to Shehar from Rockstar at first you think the song is not good but it takes over you after some listening. The lyrics are mad but the tune is catchy.
