Jai Nepal. The government has just increased the load shedding hours, though how much we lament and cuss the government and Nepal Electricity Authority (NEA) we are going to face this power cut. Every two weeks it will increase till it gets to agonizing 16 or even 18 hours a day but we will adjust and we don't even have a choice :(.
